How to use bot:

  • Tap on the button "Category" and choose category you need
  • Tap on the button "Add to Basket" under product you need
  • Tap on the button "Back to main menu..." and go to the basket(button "Basket")
  • Here you can change count of products in your basket or delete some product
  • Tap on the button "Confirm order" and bot will send invoice to you
  • Tap on the button "Test pay"(for smartphones only) and use test card number(work only with test card number) 4242 4242 4242 4242
  • You can watch history of your order, just tap on the button "Orders" in main menu
  • You can watch some info about company, just tap on the button "About"
  • You can change some info about yourself(Some info bot using like shipping address, so that info must be entered before you make orders)
